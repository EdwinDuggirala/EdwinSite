Young the Giant

Young the Giant: Indie Rock band formed in Irvine, California started in 2004. Originally, the group was called “The Jakes” but later changed their name to Young the Giant circa 2009. Their most recognizable album is their debut album titled “Young the Giant”. It released digitally in late 2010 and physically early 2011. Their first album includes hit songs like “My Body”, “Apartment”, and “I Got”. The group has transformed over the years and is always trying to come up with new sounds. Their latest project “Act I: Origins” released earlier this year and has something completely different to offer than their previous works.

Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar: rapper from Compton, California. Many people consider Kendrick Lamar to be one of the best rappers of this generation, if not the best. He has numerous awards under his belt including Grammys (2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2022), the Juno Award for international album of the year(2018), and MTV Video Music Awards (2017). 2 of his best, and my favorite albums, are “DAMN.” and “good kid, m.A.A.d city”. Between my friends and I, we say that Kendrick Lamar had the best rap album of the 2010’s. I don’t think there would be too much of an argument from anyone.
